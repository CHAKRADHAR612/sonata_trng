cronjob,solr and smartedit:

what is a cronjob?
to compute a task at a specific time

the purpose of cronjob is to execute specific business requirement on a specified interval

the interval can be event based or time based

a job to clean some files

are cronjobs and jobs both same?
from technical point of view it is not same

execute a work is a job
cronjob is an instance of a job which starts executing it

what is a trigger?
something of a task that executes periodically
work of the trigger is to execute the cronjobs periodically

it is the trigger that makes the cronjob execute periodically

job is the definition of the work
cronjob is the execution of the work
trigger is the work at which the cronjob has to execute

what is service layer job?
a job where we assign our custom business logic
